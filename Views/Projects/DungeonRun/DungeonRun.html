Dungeon Run!

My university dissertation project. Mark acheived: 67%

This game was created as my final year project for university using the Unity game engine.

This heavily inspired by games such as: The Binding of Isaac, Enter the Gungeon and Dungeon Souls this is a 3D dungeon crawler.

The aim is to fight your way through rooms and collect items (which are randomised!) once you feel you have enough power you can decide to fight the boss. If you defeat the final boss, you win!
